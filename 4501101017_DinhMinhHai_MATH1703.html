1. Đặt vấn đề (Introduction)¶

Mi lin h gia lng bc x mt tri vi yu t m mi trng v cng quan trng cho nhu cu khai thc v chuyn ha nh sng thnh in nng. N gip chng ta d on ngun cung bc x da trn m ti chnh khu vc o c. Xa hn na, nu tp d liu ln, vic xy dng mt m hnh tin on tr lng nh sng mt tri quanh nm ti mt khu vc c th l rt kh thi, t cung cp cc bng chng xc thc tnh hp l khi khi cng xy dng nng trng in mt tri ti a phng. Trong tiu lun ny, da trn tp d liu "Solar Radiation Prediction"(Andrey,2017) ti s truy tm quy lut ph thuc ca bc x mt tri vo m bng mt s cc m hnh hi quy vi hy vng tm ra cch bin din s ph thuc trn mt cch chnh xc nht.

2. Cơ sở toán học (Preliminaries)¶

Cc m hnh ti s dng phn tch tp d liu gm c hi quy tuyn tnh v hi quy a thc.

2.1. Ước lượng bình phương tối thiểu của tham số hồi quy tuyến tính¶

Phng trnh hi quy tuyn tnh n gin (simple regression equation) l \begin{align} Y = \alpha + \beta x + e \end{align} Trong $x$ l gi tr ca bin c lp, $Y$ l l gi tr tr v v $e$ l sai s c trung bnh l $0$

Ban u, chng ta ch c gi tr u vo $x_i$ v gi tr u ra $Y_i$, $i = 1,\dots,n$. xc nh mi quan h tuyn tnh gia $x_i$ v $Y_i$, ta cn c lng cc tham s $\alpha$ v $\beta$. Khi , nu $A$, $B$ ln lt l c lng ca $\alpha$ v $\beta$ th gi tr tr v thng qua phng php c lng trn ca bin $x_i$ l $A+Bx_i$. im mu cht cn ch tm nht ca phng php c lng $\alpha$ v $\beta$ l lm sao gi tr c lng $A+Bx_i$ khng sai lch nhiu vi gi tr chnh xc $Y_i$. iu ny ng ngha vi hiu $[Y_i - (A + Bx_i)]^2$ cn t gi tr nh nht. Mt khc, v ta ang c lng $A$ v $B$ sao cho phng trnh hi quy tuyn tnh m t ng nht mi lin h trn ton tp d liu $(x_i, Y_i)$, $i = 1,\dots,n$ nn s sai khc tng ng vi tng cp $(x_i,Y_i)$, lc ny, cn t gi tr hp l sao cho tng bnh phng cc sai lch $\sum_{i=1}^n [Y_i - (A + Bx_i)]^2$ t c gi tr nh nht.

Tng kt li, vi $A$, $B$ ln lt l c lng ca $\alpha$, $\beta$ v \begin{align} SSE = \sum_{i=1}^n[Y_i-(A+Bx_i)]^2 \end{align} Yu cu lc ny ca bi ton l tm $A$ v $B$ sao cho $SSE$ nh nht.

Định lý 1.1 (Định lý về ước lượng bình phương tối thiểu của tham số hồi quy)¶

c lng bnh phng ti thiu ca $\beta$ v $\alpha$ ng vi tp d liu $x_i$, $Y_i$, $i = 1,\dots,n$ l \begin{align*} &B = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n x_i Y_i - \bar{x}\sum_{i=1}^n Y_i}{\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2 - n\bar{x}}\newline &A = \bar{Y}-B\bar{x} \end{align*} ng thng $A+Bx$ c gi l ng hi quy (Ross, 2004).

Chng minh
Vi $A$, $B$ ln lt l c lng ca $\alpha$, $\beta$ v \begin{align*} SSE = \sum_{i=1}^n[Y_i-(A+Bx_i)]^2 \end{align*} Ta tnh o hm ring theo $A$ v theo $B$ c kt qu nh sau \begin{align*} \frac{dSSE}{dA}= \sum_{i=1}^n[Y_i-(A+Bx_i)] \end{align*} \begin{align*} \frac{dSSE}{dB}= \sum_{i=1}^n x_i[Y_i-(A+Bx_i)] \end{align*} Cho \begin{align*} &\frac{dSSE}{dA}=0\newline \Longrightarrow &\sum_{i=1}^n[Y_i-(A+Bx_i)] = 0\newline \Longleftrightarrow &A = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^nY_i - \frac{B}{r}\sum_{i=1}^n x_i \end{align*} Khi ny, t $\bar{Y} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^nY_i, \bar{x} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n x_i $, ta c kt qu rt gn \begin{align*} A = \bar{Y} - B\bar{x} \end{align*} Tng t, ta cho \begin{align*} &\frac{dSSE}{dB}=0\newline \Longrightarrow &\sum_{i=1}^n x_i[Y_i-(A+Bx_i)] = 0\newline \Longleftrightarrow &\sum_{i=1}^nx_iY_i -A\sum_{i=1}^nx_i - B\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2 = 0\newline \Longleftrightarrow &\sum_{i=1}^nx_iY_i +(B\bar{x}-\bar{Y})\sum_{i=1}^nx_i - B\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2 = 0\newline \Longleftrightarrow &B\left(\bar{x}\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2 - \sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2\right) = \bar{Y}\sum_{i=1}^nx_i - \sum_{i=1}^nx_iY_i \newline \Longleftrightarrow &B = \frac{\bar{Y}\sum_{i=1}^nx_i - \sum_{i=1}^nx_iY_i}{\bar{x}\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2 - \sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2} \end{align*} Vy $B = \frac{\bar{Y}\sum_{i=1}^nx_i - \sum_{i=1}^nx_iY_i}{\bar{x}\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2 - \sum_{i=1}^nx_i^2}$, $A = \bar{Y} - B\bar{x}$. Ta kt thc chng minh.

2.2. Hồi quy đa thức (Polynomial Regression)¶

$\newcommand \tab{\text{ }}$ Phng trnh hi quy a thc c dng \begin{align} Y = \beta_0 + \beta_1 x^1 + \beta_2 x^2 + \cdots + \beta_r x^r + e \end{align} Trong , $\beta_0, \beta_1, \beta_2, \dots \beta_r$ l cc h s hi quy cn tm v $e$ l sai s trung bnh bng $0$.

Ta gi s tp d liu gm cc cp $(x_i,Y_i), i = 1, 2,\dots, n$. c lng bnh phng ti thiu ca cc tham s $\beta_0, \beta_1, \beta_2, \dots \beta_r$ s l cc gi tr $B_0, B_1, B_2, \dots B_r$ tha mn tng bnh phng $SSEP$ t gi tr nh nht vi \begin{align} SSEP = \sum_{i=1}^n(Y_i- B_0- B_1x_i- B_2x_i^2-\dots - B_rx_i^r)^2 \end{align}

xc nh cc c lng trn, cng vic u tin l tnh ton o hm ring ln lt theo $B_0, B_1, B_2, \dots B_r$. Sau cho cc o hm ny bng $0$, ta s thu c cc phng trnh bn di \begin{align*} \sum_{i=1}^n & Y_i = & &B_0n + B_1\sum_{i=1}^n x_i + B_2\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2 + \cdots + & &B_r\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^r\newline \sum_{i=1}^n x_i & Y_i = & &B_0\sum_{i=1}^n x_i + B_1\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2 + B_2\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^3 + \cdots + & &B_r\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^{r+1}\newline \sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2 & Y_i = & &B_0\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2 + B_1\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^3 + B_2\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^4 + \cdots + & &B_r\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^{r+2}\newline \sum_{i=1}^n x_i^3 & Y_i = & &B_0\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^3 + B_1\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^4 + B_2\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^5 + \cdots + & &B_r\sum_{i=1}^nx_i^{r+3}\newline & \hspace{-0.5cm}\vdots & &\hspace{1cm}\vdots & & \hspace{0.9cm}\vdots \newline \sum_{i=1}^n x_i^r & Y_i = & &B_0\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^r + B_1\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^{r+1} + B_2\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^{r+2} + \cdots + & &B_r\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^{2r} \end{align*}

S dng chng lp thnh h $(r+1)$ phng trnh bc nht vi cc n $B_0, B_1, B_2, \dots B_r$. D thy h ny gii c mt cch d dng. \begin{align*} \text{Khi , vi cc h s $B_0, B_1, B_2, \dots B_r$ va tm c, ng cong } Y = B_0 + B_1 x^1 + B_2 x^2 + \cdots + B_r x^r \text{ chnh l ng hi quy} \end{align*}

3.1 Tập dữ liệu "Solar Radiation Prediction" và những tinh chỉnh¶

"Solar Radiation Prediction" l tp d liu kh tng c thu thp ti trm thi tit HI-SEAS(t ti sn ni la Mauna Loa) trong 4 thng (t thng 9 n thng 12 nm 2016). Cc n v d liu chnh ca tp d liu bao gm:

• Bc x mt tri (watt trn mt vung)
• Nhit ( F)
• m (phn trm)
• p sut kh quyn (Hg)
• Hng gi ()
• Tc gi (dm(miles) mi gi)
• Thi im mt tri mc/ln (da theo gi Hawaii)

Da trn m t ca ngi cung cp d liu, ti c bit 2 yu t "Bc x mt tri" v " m" c lin quan n nhau. Tht vy, khi nh sng truyn qua khng kh, chng c th b hp th, b tn x hoc phn x bi cc phn t kh, hi nc, my,...(Solar Energy Technologies Office, n.d.). Do ,khi nhn thc c gia chng c mt tng lin h ti bt tay vo s dng cc m hnh hi quy tuyn tnh v hi quy a thc phn tch nhm d liu bc x- m vi gi nh chng c mt mi lin h tuyn tnh hoc mt mi lin h bc cao no .

V hn ch trong kh nng lp trnh, ti khng thao tc trc tip trn tp d liu gc(Solar Radiation Prediction). Thay vo , ti s dng cc cng c ca phn mm Excel sao cho tnh c tng bc x $SoR$ v tng phn trm m $SoH$ t lc mt tri mc n thi im ln (t 6h05 n 18h35 mi ngy) sau chia ly trung bnh v nhn c mt tp d liu mi gm: trung bnh m trong mt ln o(Mean of Humidity)- gi n l bin c lp $X$ v trung bnh bc x trong mt ln o (Mean of Solar_Radiation)- gi l bin ph thuc $Y$.C th \begin{align*} X = \frac{SoH}{n}, Y =\frac{SoR}{n} \end{align*} vi $n$ chnh l s ln o. Lc ny, ti c mt tp d liu mi gm 2 ct: m, bc x v 116 d liu mi ct, tng ng vi tng s ngy trong 4 thng (thng 9, 10, 11, 12) b i cc ngy 30/09, 30/11, 6/12, 7/12 do khng c d liu v cc ngy 29/10 v 29/11 (do s liu trung bnh m cao bt thng (133% vo ngy 29/10)).

from matplotlib.ticker import PercentFormatter
def graph_by_color(str_X_e):
    X_e = df[str_X_e]
    fig, axs = plt.subplots(1, 1, tight_layout=True)
    n_bins = 20
    # N is the count in each bin, bins is the lower-limit of the bin
    N, bins, patches = axs.hist(X_e, bins=n_bins)

    # We'll color code by height, but you could use any scalar
    fracs = N / N.max()

    # we need to normalize the data to 0..1 for the full range of the colormap
    norm = colors.Normalize(fracs.min(), fracs.max())

    # Now, we'll loop through our objects and set the color of each accordingly
    for thisfrac, thispatch in zip(fracs, patches):
        color = plt.cm.viridis(norm(thisfrac))
        thispatch.set_facecolor(color)
    axs.set_xlabel(str_X_e)
    axs.set_ylabel('Frequency')
    return None

Mt cch trc quan nhn bit m hnh hi quy c chnh xc trong vic gii thch tp d liu cho hay khng l da vo standardized residuals(tm dch: s d chun, gi tt l $SR$). Nu m hnh hi quy l ng th cc i lng s d $Y_i - (A + Bx_i), i = 1, 2, \dots, n$ sau khi c chun ha (to thnh $SR$) s xp x mt bin ngu nhin c lp c phn phi chun. Do , khi minh ha trn th $SR$ nn phn b ngu nhin xung quanh ng thng $y=0$ v $95\%$ trong s chng nn nm gia $2$ v $-2$ (v $P\{-1.96<Z<1.96\}=0.95$)(Ross, 2004).

4. Kết quả (Results)¶

Trong chng trnh trn, kt qu nhn c khi p dng cc m hnh hi quy tuyn tnh v hi quy a thc d on lng bc x mt tri da trn m cho trc ($30\%$ tp d liu) khng my kh quan. Gi tr $R^2$(Coefficient of detemination) ln lt l $0.636$ cho m hnh hi quy a thc v $0.482$ cho m hnh hi quy tuyn tnh.

5. Bàn luận (Discussion)¶

M hnh hi quy tuyn tnh v hi quy a thc khng hiu qu trong d on bc x nh sng thng qua m mi trng, v th ta khng th s dng chng lm c s d bo ngun cung nng lng mt tri ti a phng, cng nh l bin chng thnh bng chng hp l ha tng xy dng vng sn xut in mt tri tp trung.

Mc d chng ta tha nhn cc m hnh hi quy c dng trong bi vit ny khng tt khi d on tp d liu nhng vi cc kt qu m n mang li, ti c th khng nh mi tng quan tng i mnh gia bc x v m. iu ny cng c li gi nh ban u m ti ngm tha nhn t ngi cung cp d liu.

i vi cc nghin cu trong tng lai, ti mun xut s dng thm ct d liu "p sut kh quyn" vo m hnh bi mt cc phn t kh trong mi trng lm tc ng n s suy gim ca bc x. iu ny ging nh bi hc t m: "Cc phn t nc trong khng kh cng nhiu, tc m cng cao th mc bc x ca mt tri o c ti cng gim". Ngoi ra, ti cng rt ch i d liu cp nht c th cung cp thm mt bi trong khng gian cc thi im o c. Cng nhiu yu t nh hng hay c kh nng nh hng n s di chuyn ca nh sng trong khng gian. S c mt ca n s gip ti c th bao ph, lm hp i cc im m v mt d liu t ci thin c m hnh hi quy ca minh.
